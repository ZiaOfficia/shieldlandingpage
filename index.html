Welcome to Shield

Where Dreams of Wearing the Uniform Turn into Reality

Established in 2015, Shield Defence Academy has emerged as one of Lucknow's most trusted institutions for defence aspirants. Every year, more than 150 candidates step through our doors with a single dream — to wear the uniform — and a strong majority of them go on to be recommended for the Armed Forces.

Under the leadership of Founder Director Shivam Shukla, our training goes far beyond textbooks. We focus on personality development, real exam readiness, and the discipline that defines an officer. Written exam coaching for NDA, CDS, AFCAT, Air Force X/Y, Navy and Sainik School is integrated with end-to-end SSB preparation — Screening, Psychology, GTO and Interview — all under one roof.

With three campuses across Lucknow, smart classrooms, India's largest GTO ground, hostel facilities and a panel of retired SSB board officers, Shield is engineered for one purpose: to prepare candidates who don't just clear exams, but who are worthy of leading the nation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Want to Know

Which academy is best for defence preparation in Lucknow? +

Shield Defence Academy is widely regarded among the top choices for NDA, CDS, AFCAT and SSB preparation in Lucknow, with experienced faculty, retired SSB board members and a strong consistent selection record.

Does Shield Defence Academy offer schooling along with NDA coaching? +

Yes. Through the residential NDA Foundation programme, students from Classes 9 to 12 can pursue regular schooling along with structured NDA preparation in a single disciplined campus.

Which courses are offered at Shield Defence Academy? +

Coaching is offered for NDA / NA, NDA Foundation (with schooling), CDS, AFCAT, SSB Interview, Sainik School entrance, Air Force X / Y group and Navy AA / SSR — with offline, online and hybrid modes.

Is hostel accommodation available? +

Yes. The residential campus provides safe rooms, hygienic food, disciplined routines and academic supervision — ideal for outstation aspirants.

What are the NDA coaching fees? +

Fees are structured, transparent and affordable, covering classroom sessions, study material, mock tests and SSB training. Flexible payment options and hostel packages are available — please connect with the admissions team for the latest rates.

Where is the academy located? +

Head Office: Beg Tower, Ayodhya Road, Shakti Nagar, Indira Nagar, Lucknow — near Lekhraj Metro Station. Branch Office: Sita Arcade (2nd Floor), opposite Awadh Hospital, Alambagh, Lucknow. Residential Campus: Faizabad Road, Anora Kala, Lucknow.

What is the admission procedure? +

You can apply online or visit any of the campuses. Admission includes form submission, counselling and course selection. After enrolment, students are given a structured study plan, study material and access to all preparation resources.
